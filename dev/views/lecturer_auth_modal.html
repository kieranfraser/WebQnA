Welcome to the authorisation process for lecturers. The steps are simple:

Step 1
Click the button below to send your user details to the Qanda team.
Step 2
A member of the Qanda team will contact you to verify your position within your academic institution.
Step 3
Once verification is complete your user authorisation level will be upgraded to "Lecturer".
What this will mean:
- You will have the ability to add classes and control posts in your class.
- You will have access to class statistics.
- You will be able to see all anonymously posted information.
- You will have access to "Lecture Resources" where all lecturers can archive and share teaching material.

User details sent. Qanda will be in touch shortly to verify your identity.

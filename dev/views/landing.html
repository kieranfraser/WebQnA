Social Media meets the Classroom

ACCOUNTS

Lecturers have access to special accounts, from which they can moderate class discussions.

Q&A

Student and lecturers can participate in questions and answer sessions.

PRIVACY

Students can interact with other students and respond to questions anonymously.

Existing Problems

Students do not interact with the lecturer for fear of asking a silly question.

Lecturers struggle because they have no idea if students understood the recently taught material.

Future lecturers of a particular module, have no way of determining which parts were successful in the past.

Proposed Solutions

Our app allows students to participate in anonymous Q&A sessions with the lecturer.

Lecturers can create polls and pose questions for the students to answer.

Future lecturers of the module can access Q&A sessions from previous years.
